pesto-chicken-penne-casserole

This is a delicious recipe for a pesto chicken penne casserole. It is inexpensive, does not take too many ingredients, and is easy to make./

Ingredients

  • Bread Crumbs
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Olive Oil
  • Penne Pasta
  • Cooked Chicken
  • Italian Cheese
  • Baby Spinach
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Alfredo Sauce
  • Pesto Sauce
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F and grease a baking pan. Combine the bread crumb, parmesan cheese, and olive oil in a bowl for a later step.
  2. Fill a pot with salted water and boil. Stir in penne pasta and stir occasionally for 11 minutes, then drain.
  3. Combine the set aside chicken, cheese, spinach, tomatoes, Alfredo sauce, Pesto sauce, and milk. Then stir in pasta and top with bread crumbs.
  4. Bake in oven for around 45 minutes (should be golden brown on top)
