chili-dog-mac-and-cheese

This is a recipe for a delicious Chili Dog Mac and Cheese meal. While it may take a while to prepare and cook, it is well worth the effort. It should take around 20 minutes to prep and 1 hr 20 mins to cook. It should serve around 12 people.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Ground Beef
  • Large Onion
  • Kosher Salt
  • Chili Powder
  • Ground Cumin
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Ground Black Pepper
  • Ground Cinnamon
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Dried Oregano
  • Garlic
  • Green Peppers
  • Hot Dogs
  • Canned Crushed Tomatoes
  • Water
  • Elbow Macaroni
  • White Cheddar Cheese
  • Monterey Jack Cheese
  • Green Onions

Steps

  1. Add olive oil to pot and heat on high. Then add beef and cook/stir for two minutes. Add salt and onions, cooking and stirring around 5 minutes.
  2. Add chili powder, cumin, paprika, black pepper, cinnamon, cayenne peppers, dried oregano, and garlic. Then cook for two minutes while stirring.
  3. Add peppers, hot dogs, tomatoes, and water. Stir it all together and simmer for around 30 minutes
  4. Fill a pot with salted water and boil. Add macaroni and stir for 10 minutes.
  5. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F
  6. Remove chili from heat, drain water from macaroni pot and add the macaroni to the chili pot. Stir accordingly and add 60% of the cheese and stir in.
  7. Add the rest of the cheese to the top and bake in oven for 35 minutes
  8. Remove from oven and let rest for 15 minutes.
