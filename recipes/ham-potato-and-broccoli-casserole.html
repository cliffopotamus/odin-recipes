ham-potato-and-broccoli-casserole

Ingredients

  • French Fries
  • Broccoli
  • Ham
  • Cream of Mushroom Soup
  • Milk
  • Mayonnaise
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Spray baking pan with cooking spray, then add french fries to the bottom. Then add in broccoli and ham in that order.
  3. Mix mayonnaise, milk, and soup altogether in a small bowl and pour over baking dish.
  4. Bake in oven for 40 minutes.
